How the App Works

  1. Order and pay from your phone

    ORDER AT THE BOUTIQUE
    Skip the line!

    When you arrive at the boutique, open the app on your phone and place your order. You'll be called by the name you registered with when it’s ready.

    SCHEDULE A PICKUP
    Select your items, choose your preferred pickup time and you’re set. Tap the in-app banner upon arrival so we can prepare your order fresh.

  2. Join the AMORINO CLUB

    Download the app, create your account and receive a welcome treat: a Gianduiotto bonbon with gelato after your first order over €10.

  3. Earn Petals with your purchases

    Earn 10 Petals for every €1 spent via the app or kiosks.

  4. Redeem Rewards

    Use your collected Petals to claim items from the Amorino menu.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I earn and redeem Petals at any Amorino location?

Check the app for participating boutiques in Spain.

How do I place an order with the app?

Open the app, choose a boutique, select your items, pick a time and pay.

How do I collect my order?

If ordering now, you’ll be called when it’s ready. If you scheduled it, tap the arrival banner in the app.

What payment methods can I use?

Credit card, Apple Pay or Google Pay directly in the app.

How do I earn loyalty points?

Make purchases via the app or scan your Club QR code at kiosks.

How do I redeem my Petals?

As you reach each level, you’ll be able to claim rewards via the app.

What rewards are available?

Toppings, macarons, Italian coffee, gelato, crepes and more.
