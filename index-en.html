Frequently Asked Questions

Can I earn and redeem Petals at any Amorino location in Spain?

You can use your loyalty rewards at most participating Amorino locations in Spain.

Check the app home screen to view the list of boutiques offering the service.

How do I place an order with the app?

It’s super easy!

Just open the app, select a boutique, place your order, choose if you want it now or later, and pay right from your phone.

How do I collect my order?

Ordering via the app means no more waiting at the kiosk or counter.

If you selected “immediate order,” it will go directly into the order queue, and you’ll be called by name when it’s ready.

If you scheduled a pickup time, just tap the confirmation banner in the app when you arrive at the boutique.

What payment methods can I use?

Browse the full menu from your phone, select your favorites, and pay securely with your credit/debit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay.

How do I earn loyalty points?

Once you’ve created an account in the app, you’ll be enrolled in Amorino’s loyalty program.

You’ll earn petals (points) for every purchase made through the app.

Additionally, you can also earn petals at the boutique kiosks by scanning your Club Amorino member QR code before payment (available under the Loyalty tab in the app).

How can I redeem my Petals?

As you collect petals, you’ll unlock new loyalty levels:

Bronze
200 petals

Silver
500 petals

Gold
900 petals

Platinum
1300 petals

Each level offers different rewards. To redeem a reward:

  1. Go to Loyalty and claim the reward. It will appear under your Offers tab.
  2. Select the offer.
  3. GIf using a kiosk, tap the offer to display a QR code. Before payment, in the cart screen where you enter your name, indicate you’re a Club Amorino member and scan the code.
  4. If using the app, just add the reward to your cart before checking out.
Note: Only one reward can be redeemed per order.

What gifts are available?

There’s a selection of our most popular Amorino products: toppings, macarons, Italian coffees, and of course, our amazing gelato, waffles, and crêpes.

You can view the full list under the Loyalty tab in the app by tapping each loyalty level.
